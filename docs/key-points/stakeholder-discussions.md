### Purpose
To **clarify requirements, expectations, and priorities** directly with stakeholders to ensure the project aligns with business goals.

### Focus
Facilitates **effective communication** between stakeholders and the project team to reduce misunderstandings.

### Audience
- Project Managers  
- Business Analysts  
- Developers  
- QA Engineers  
- Clients / Business Stakeholders

### Key Activities
- Gather feedback on requirements and objectives  
- Confirm **priority of features and modules**  
- Identify **constraints, dependencies, and assumptions**  
- Discuss success criteria and key performance indicators (KPIs)  
- Resolve conflicts or ambiguities in requirements

### Benefits
- Ensures **shared understanding** of project goals  
- Reduces **rework and scope creep** during development  
- Builds **stakeholder trust and engagement**  
- Helps in **setting realistic timelines and expectations**

### Format
- Meetings, workshops, or video calls  
- Minutes of Meeting (MoM) documenting decisions and actions  
- Supporting diagrams, charts, or tables if needed

### Frequency
- During **requirement gathering**  
- Before **design and development phases**  
- As needed for **major scope changes or clarifications**

### Review Process
- All discussion points should be **documented, validated, and agreed upon by stakeholders**  
- MoM should be **circulated for confirmation** to avoid misunderstandings
