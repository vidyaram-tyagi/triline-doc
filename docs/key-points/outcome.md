### Purpose
To summarize the **expected results and benefits** from proper requirement gathering, scope definition, and design preparation.

### Focus
Ensures that the project team and stakeholders have a **shared understanding of goals and deliverables**.

### Key Activities
- Consolidate **all gathered requirements**  
- Confirm **design references and project scope**  
- Document **expected deliverables and success criteria**  
- Prepare **baseline for development, testing, and deployment**

### Benefits
- Provides **clarity and alignment** across all teams  
- Reduces **misunderstandings and rework**  
- Serves as a **reference for evaluating project success**  
- Helps in **prioritizing tasks and resources**

### Format
- Summary report or document  
- Tables or bullet points for deliverables  
- Visual charts for timelines or KPIs

### Review Process
- Reviewed and validated by **project manager and stakeholders**
