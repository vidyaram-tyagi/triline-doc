### Purpose
To **clearly define the project boundaries, deliverables, and schedule** to set expectations and prevent scope creep.

### Focus
Ensures all stakeholders and team members understand **what will be delivered and when**.

### Audience
- Project Managers  
- Developers  
- QA Engineers  
- Business Analysts  
- Clients / Stakeholders

### Key Activities
- Identify **in-scope and out-of-scope items**  
- Define **major deliverables and milestones**  
- Estimate **time required for each phase or module**  
- Allocate resources and responsibilities  
- Set realistic timelines based on team capacity

### Benefits
- Provides a **clear roadmap** for development and testing  
- Helps manage **stakeholder expectations**  
- Reduces **delays and miscommunications**  
- Serves as a **baseline for progress tracking**

### Format
- Tables or charts for deliverables and deadlines  
- Gantt charts for timeline visualization  
- Supporting notes for assumptions or dependencies

### Review Process
- Reviewed and approved by **project manager and stakeholders** before development begins
