### Purpose
Captures the **business goals, objectives, and high-level requirements** of a project.

### Focus
Explains **why the project exists** and **what value it delivers** to the business.

### Audience
Primarily for **stakeholders, project managers, business analysts, and clients**.

### Content Includes
- Project overview and background  
- Business goals and objectives  
- Stakeholder list and responsibilities  
- High-level requirements and constraints  
- Success criteria and KPIs

### Scope
Defines **whatâ€™s in-scope and out-of-scope** for the project.

### Benefits
- Ensures **shared understanding of business needs**  
- Reduces **misunderstandings during development**  
- Serves as a **reference for all subsequent documents** (SRS, FRD)

### Format
Usually a **formal document**, sometimes supplemented with **diagrams, tables, or flowcharts**.

### Versioning
Keep track of updates with **version numbers and dates**.

### Review Process
Must be **reviewed and approved by all stakeholders** before design or development begins.
