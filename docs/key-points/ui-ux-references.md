### Purpose
To provide **visual clarity and design direction** for the development team using mockups or prototypes.

### Focus
Ensures **consistent and user-friendly interface design** aligned with project requirements.

### Audience
- UI/UX Designers  
- Developers  
- QA Engineers  
- Product Managers  
- Stakeholders

### Key Activities
- Collect design references from **Figma, Adobe XD, or sketches**  
- Create **wireframes and prototypes**  
- Review **user flows and interface layouts**  
- Gather **feedback from stakeholders** before finalizing designs

### Benefits
- Reduces **design ambiguities** during development  
- Helps **developers implement UI accurately**  
- Improves **user experience and usability**  
- Provides a **visual guide for testers** to validate UI

### Format
- Interactive prototypes (Figma links)  
- Screenshots or annotated wireframes  
- Flowcharts for navigation and interactions

### Review Process
- Reviewed and approved by **design team and stakeholders** before development
